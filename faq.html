Frequently Asked Questions

Everything we get asked most, from service area to scheduling. Don't see your question? Just reach out.

What areas do you service?

We serve Greenville, Mauldin, and Piedmont, South Carolina, and the surrounding area. Not sure if you're in range? Reach out and we'll let you know.

What's included in the Full Service Package?

Every visit includes mowing, weedeating, edging, and blowing — the whole package, every time. We don't split it up or charge extra for any of those four.

How much does lawn care cost?

Pricing depends on the size of your yard and how often you'd like service. Fill out the quote form or give us a call and we'll get you a straight answer, usually pretty quick.

Do you offer one-time cleanups, or only recurring service?

Both. We do ongoing weekly or biweekly mowing, and we also take one-time jobs like yard cleanups, mulching, hedge trimming, and small projects.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the form on our homepage or call or text us at (864) 517-9080. We'll follow up with a price, usually pretty quick.

What if it rains on my scheduled service day?

We'll reach out to reschedule for the next available day. No need to worry about it or follow up yourself.
