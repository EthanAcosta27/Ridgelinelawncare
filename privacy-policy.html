Privacy Policy

Last updated July 15, 2026

Ridgeline Lawn Care is a small, locally owned business — we're not lawyers, and this page is written in plain language to explain what we collect and why. If you have questions, just call or email us.

What We Collect

When you fill out our quote request form, we collect the information you give us: your name, phone number, email address, property address, and any details you share about your lawn or project. We don't collect this information any other way — we don't use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or ad pixels on this site.

How We Use It

We use your information for one purpose: to get back to you with a quote and, if you hire us, to schedule and provide service. We may contact you by phone, text message, or email to follow up on your request.

How It's Sent and Stored

Our quote form uses a third-party service called EmailJS to deliver your submission to our inbox as an email. EmailJS processes the form data briefly in order to send it — see their privacy policy for details on how they handle that. We don't maintain a separate customer database or CRM; your information lives in our email inbox like any other message you'd send us directly.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We don't use your information for anything beyond responding to your request and providing service.
  • We don't place tracking cookies or run ad retargeting on this site.

Google Fonts

The typefaces on this site are hosted directly on our own server rather than loaded from Google's servers, so no font-related data is sent to Google when you visit.

Your Choices

You can ask us to delete any information you've submitted at any time — just email info@ridgelinelawncare.com or call (864) 517-9080 and we'll remove it from our records.

Changes to This Policy

If we change how we handle your information, we'll update this page with a new "last updated" date above.
